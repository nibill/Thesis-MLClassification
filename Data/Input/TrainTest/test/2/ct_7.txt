trischli presents in collaboration with the bankers' club, consulting club and esprit "the wolf of dufourstreet”. 
an evening of a slightly different kind, where you can get to know consulting@hsg and the bankers club first at a meet&greet, and then can experience the clubs from a more amusing perspective. 

- “have you also bought bitcoins” 
- "have just finished consulting a company, and don't think we should invest in this sector"
- “the stock market is overheating, i wouldn't keep buying!"
- "my friend said we should at all costs invest in these small cap shares - they're meant to rise to the stars" 

consultants, bankers, bitcoin millionaires, fortune-tellers and stock players, on the 7th of march 2018 you can combine your knowledge into a beer stock exchange. the principle is simple: the price of beer is determined by the market. demand and supply regulate the price, just like in a "real" stock exchange (minus the short and long positions 😉). of course you can then keep trading beer with each-other.
