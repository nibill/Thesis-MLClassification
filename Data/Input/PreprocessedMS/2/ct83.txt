van keuken montage jan dop and johan van keuken sound nosh van lely brass unbound is the fruit of interaction between the filmmaker and anthropologist rob boonzajer flaes it tells the tale of brass instruments a symbol of the cultural yoke imposed by colonial forces from indonesia to nepal from ghana to surinam and their appropriation by colonized populations a musical escapade above all the film recounts how the instruments freed themselves from their masters were diverted from christianity which had imported them and became the cherished captives of conquered countries indispensable accompaniment to the festivities and rituals of polytheistic religion from country to country it shows how the vestiges of colonialism were imbedded all public and private events the saga of a large bugle s travels across the country across time followed gleefully by the camera is a slice of anthology a musical celebration combined with a moment of pure cinematographic pleasure synopsis from
